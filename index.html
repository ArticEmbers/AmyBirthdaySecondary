The Group

A Force That Changed Everything

BIGBANG emerged from YG Entertainment in 2006 as five young men with an extraordinary vision: to fuse hip-hop, R&B, pop and electronic music into something that the world had never heard before.

Over nearly two decades, they shattered records, built global fanbases, influenced fashion, broke cultural barriers, and produced some of the most iconic music in Korean pop history — while each member simultaneously carved out a legendary solo career.

G-Dragon, Taeyang, Daesung, T.O.P, and Seungri didn't just become stars, they became cultural institutions. Their influence reverberates through every generation of K-Pop that followed.

G-Dragon

Kwon Ji-yong · 권지용

Rapper Producer Fashion Icon Songwriter Creative Director

Kwon Ji-yong, who is also known as G-Dragon is a big deal. He is not a musician. G-Dragon is an artist who is changing things for his generation. His music and style are loved by people around the world. G-Dragon was born on August 18 1988 in Seoul. When he was six years old he started working with YG Entertainment. G-Dragon trained with them for, than ten years. Then he became the leader of BIGBANG and the main person who comes up with the groups ideas and music.

As the leader and main songwriter of the group G-Dragon has written most of BIGBANGs songs. He also has a solo career. His first solo album was Heartbreaker in 2009. Then he released Coup d'Etat and KWON JIYONG, which are considered important projects. These projects made G-Dragon a well-known and respected creative person, in K-Pop. G-Dragon and BIGBANG are still popular today.

The Legacy

What BIGBANG Built

Before we had BTS before we had EXO there was this big group called BIGBANG. They were the ones who showed everyone that music from Korea could be huge everywhere. I mean they could sell out stadiums in every part of the world. BIGBANG proved that a musician could be super famous in Korea and still be really important in cities like New York, Paris and São Paulo. BIGBANG was a deal and they helped make Korean music popular all, over the world with BIGBANG leading the way.

🌊
The Wave Makers
BIGBANG was one of the K-Pop groups to really make it big in the West. They helped open doors for groups. Their 2012 Alive Galaxy Tour made over $100 million worldwide. BIGBANG showed that K-Pop could be a success, in markets.
👗
Fashion Architects
G-Dragons impact on fashion is huge. He worked with brands like Chanel, NIKE and his own label Peaceminusone. This helped make K-Pop a major player in fashion. Korean style is now part of the luxury fashion talk around the world thanks to G-Dragon. He made K-Pop and Korean aesthetics important in the fashion world. G-Dragons fashion influence is still going strong. K-Pop and fashion are now. G-Dragon is a big reason, for that.
🎵
Musical Architects
From songs like "Fantastic Baby" that have a feel to emotional songs like "Blue" and "Haru Haru " BIGBANG changed the sound of K-Pop. They added styles like hip-hop, EDM, R&B and rock to K-Pop music. BIGBANG made these styles fit well with K-Pop. BIGBANG made K-Pop sound more interesting by mixing types of music. They made songs that're energetic and songs that are emotional. BIGBANGs music includes hip-hop, EDM, R&B and rock. These styles are part of what makes K-Pop, from BIGBANG special. The group made K-Pop with a mix of styles. BIGBANG is known for their K-Pop music that has influences.
🏟️
Concert Legends
Their concert shows. With fireworks flying stages and huge LED screens. Changed what a K-Pop concert can be. They set a bar, for concert productions that made other K-Pop concerts look different. K-Pop concerts now have better shows. The K-Pop concerts have more to offer with these productions. K-Pop fans love the exciting live shows.
🎬
Multi-Dimensional Artists
BIGBANG was different from idol groups because each member of BIGBANG did their own thing. T.O.P did some acting. G-Dragon got into fashion. Taeyang became really good at dance. Daesung became a star, in Japan. This shows that the members of BIGBANG were artists, not just people who perform. BIGBANG members were talented in areas.
🌏
VIP Nation
Their fans, called VIPs are super loyal. Have been with BIGBANG for a long time. When the band took breaks had to do military service or were away, for years their fans stuck around. This shows how much BIGBANGs music and art really mean to their VIPs. The fans love BIGBANG and BIGBANGs music much. BIGBANGs fans are an example of how music can bring people together.

The Journey

A Decade of History

2006
BIGBANG Debut
Five young artists debut under YG Entertainment, immediately signaling something different with their hip-hop infused sound and raw energy.
2008
Haru Haru Era
The release of Stand Up and the iconic ballad Haru Haru establishes BIGBANG as more than performers — they become storytellers.
2011
Japan Conquest
BIGBANG explodes in Japan, selling out domes and achieving what no K-Pop group had matched — becoming legitimate J-Pop superstars.
2012
ALIVE Galaxy Tour
The ALIVE mini-album and its accompanying global tour grosses over $100 million. Fantastic Baby goes viral worldwide. K-Pop will never be the same.
2015
MADE Series
A groundbreaking single-release strategy produces Loser, Bae Bae, Bang Bang Bang — every song a #1 hit, every release an event.
2016
MADE Full Album
Their first full Korean album. Hailed as a masterpiece. FXXK IT and Last Dance become era-defining anthems before military service begins.
2022
Still Life
After years of military service and waiting, BIGBANG returns with "Still Life" — a bittersweet, mature anthem that proves their power remains undimmed.
